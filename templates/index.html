The issue

In Australia, 87% of parents and carers are worried about being able to buy school materials and another 40% think their children will miss out on school supplies. Australian children are being burdened by cost-of-living pressures, and it's denying them their right to a quality education. Most textbooks cost almost $100, and for VCE students who really need textbooks to complete their studies, it's a big ask.

Textbooks are distributed under strict licenses that don't allow them to be easily accessed online. For many students, the only affordable means of accessing their textbooks is via the use of piracy sites and PDF copies distributed by others. The big problem with this is that it becomes very difficult to find the most up-to-date textbook. PDF copies also lack interactivity, reducing the overall engagement of the textbook.

Survey of 31 VCE students (2024)

The solution

Openbook is a free textbook platform for VCE. Every textbook on Openbook is a subject-specific open textbook. Open textbooks are published with open licenses that allow anyone to access them and suggest edits. Openbook Textbook Engine (OTE) is the software built to write Openbook textbooks compatible with the online platform. Authors can continuously collaborate on textbooks to ensure they stay up-to-date and relevant. OTE also provides special textbook templating features to support interactive questions and video embeds.

Most importantly, Openbook is free to use for everyone due to its open-source nature. That means every student is able to access valuable educational content without any burden of cost.
